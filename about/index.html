Irene Iodice

I am a PhD student enrolled in a program of cotutelle between Sant'Anna School of Advanced Studies and Université Paris 1 Panthéon-Sorbonne. My research interests include industrial organization, empirical analysis of firm performance and its link with diversification strategies, micro and non-parametric econometrics, and the dynamics of international trade. My PhD thesis is part of a research project in which we are developing several instruments to treat and study multiproducts firms as products vectors.
